ABOUT

E-CELL

Founded with the vision of fostering entrepreneurship and innovation, the Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) at SMVIT has grown into a dynamic community of passionate individuals. Initially re-established in 2021 by a dedicated group of seniors, the club has thrived as a platform for students eager to explore the world of startups, networking, and business development. In just three years, our E-Cell has achieved significant milestones. We proudly represented SMVIT at prestigious competitions and events, including the National Entrepreneurship Challenges (NEC) hosted by IIT Madras and IIT Bombay. At the IIT Bombay NEC, our team secured an impressive 16th place, showcasing our commitment to excellence and growth on a national scale. Beyond competitions, our members have attended startup expos, such as Startup Melas, where we networked with industry leaders and gained insights into the latest trends in the startup ecosystem. On campus, the E-Cell organizes a variety of events designed to nurture entrepreneurial skills and encourage innovative thinking. We’ve hosted pitching competitions that allow students to present their startup ideas, receive valuable feedback, and even gain mentorship from industry experts. Additionally, our events foster essential skills, from ideation and business planning to effective pitching and networking, ensuring our members are well-prepared for real-world challenges. Looking ahead, our mission remains the same: to build a community of like-minded individuals driven by creativity and ambition. The E-Cell is not just a club but a launchpad for those with dreams of creating and leading ventures that make an impact. Join us, and be part of a community that believes in turning ideas into reality!

OUR MISSION


Our mission is to create a self-sustaining campus hub that fosters entrepreneurship by connecting students with industry experts, understanding market demands, and providing hands-on training to launch start-ups seamlessly

OUR VISION


Our vision is to create a self-sustaining club that supports entrepreneurs through workshops, innovation labs, and networking opportunities like ideathons and hackathons. We aim to host an annual E-Summit, building partnerships, generating revenue, and showcasing our ability to manage large-scale projects. Additionally, we will offer micro-grant programs, on-campus incubators, and a strong alumni network for mentorship, investment, and partnerships to support ventures beyond campus.
